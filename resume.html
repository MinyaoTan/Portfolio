The goal was to explore the potential of prosthetic hand controlled by arm muscle contraction. This mechanism was powered by 9-volt batteries and the source of control was muscle contraction signal from the user. At the end of the project, the robotic hand was able to produce consistent grasping motion based on the magnitude of the live contraction signal.
